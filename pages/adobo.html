ADOBO

Decription

Considered by some as the pride of Filipino cuisine, Pork Adobo is definitely a favorite among many around the world. For this, we cook pork slices in a perfect mix of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic to make this savory and sour viand. We just need to combine our ingredients, and let them simmer for some time. Making adobo really is not as difficult as it may seem!

Marinating

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Then bring your mixture to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
  5. Put the vinegar inside and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 head garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tsp peppercorn
  • 2 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
